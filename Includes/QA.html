Common Questions

What is a "consultation meeting" and why do we need it?

Consultation before buying - after viewing our website and finding the desired vehicle, you can schedule a consultation meeting with us in order to receive additional information for the vehicle you have chosen,in addition we will give you the most important criteria and give you a hand to make a final decision.

How can I schedule a consultation?

Click on the "Consultation meeting tab" or on the button

then enter the required relevant information as it appears, and the most important thing is to save the vehicle's serial number in order to know which vehicle interests you. If there is no vehicle that interests you and you just want to consult - leave this box empty.

Where is your company office located? when do you work?

We as an Israeli company are located in Tel Aviv, our office is also located in Tel Aviv and the address is:8 Malacha St. - Tel Aviv. Opening hours: every day from 8:00 am to 9:00 pm. You can reach us through WAZE through the search on BESTCAR.

Important Questions

Is the prices of the vehicles on the website final? Is it possible to get a discount?

The prices you see on the website are final, and cannot be changed - but in special cases you can get a 5% discount, these cases are: - When buying 2 vehicles at once. - If it is found that you are regular customers (you have 2+ purchases in the past). - If you pay more than half the price in cash.

Is there a warranty on your vehicles and services?

Of course there is! We give a warranty on every car bought from our garage, the warranty varies from car to car, but don't worry - we are always with you!

Is there an "exchange" option for the car? That is to get a new car in exchange for my car.

Yes, it is possible - you must search for a vehicle whose price is almost equal to the price of your vehicle, then schedule a consultation meeting with us, we will check the condition of your vehicle and decide whether it can be exchanged or not.
